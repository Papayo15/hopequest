## üé® General Style Guidelines for All Characters

### Art Direction
- **Style**: Pixar/DreamWorks quality 3D animation style
- **Fur/Skin**: Highly detailed textures (fur should look soft, scales smooth)
- **Eyes**: Large, expressive, emotional (Pixar trademark)
- **Proportions**: Slightly stylized but believable
- **Lighting**: Soft, warm, studio lighting
- **Background**: White or transparent for easy extraction
- **Render**: High quality 3D render, not flat 2D

### Color Palette (Warm & Hopeful)
- Use saturated but not neon colors
- Warm tones for heroes (orange, green, pink, gold)
- Cool tones for obstacles (ice blue)
- Don Bowser: Exaggerated orange (satire)

### Expression Guidelines
- **Heroes**: Determined, hopeful, kind, brave
- **Teo**: Innocent, curious
- **Xolo**: Wise, calm
- **Don Bowser**: Grumpy but comical (not terrifying)
- **Koopas**: Neutral, stoic (not evil)

**Too Simple / Not Detailed Enough**:

- Be very specific: "anthropomorphic FOX character" (emphasize species)

**Wrong Color**:

## üì¶ Export Settings

When generating with AI tools:

### For Midjourney
- **Aspect Ratio**: `--ar 1:1` (square for character portraits)
- **Quality**: `--q 2` (highest quality)
- **Style**: `--style raw` (more realistic rendering)
- **Version**: Use latest model (v6+)

### For DALL-E 3
- **Size**: 1024x1024 (square) or 1792x1024 (wide for turnarounds)
- **Style**: "vivid" for colorful Pixar look
- **Quality**: "hd" for best results

### For Stable Diffusion
- **Model**: Use "Realistic Vision" or "DreamShaper" for Pixar style
- **Steps**: 50+ for quality
- **CFG Scale**: 7-9 for balanced results
- **Sampler**: DPM++ 2M Karras or Euler A

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Generate in batches**: Do all Marco poses, then Luis, etc.
2. **Keep prompt consistent**: Use similar wording for character consistency
3. **Iterate**: Start with one character, get it perfect, then apply learnings to others

5. **Mix and match**: Use Photoshop/GIMP to combine best parts from multiple generations
