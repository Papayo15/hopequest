## ğŸ“š Educational Content (15% Migration)

- Geography facts (capitals, landmarks, regions)

- Historical events and figures

### Migration Content (15%)
- **Historical Context**: Migration history of that country (sensitivity level 2-3)

## ğŸ¯ Educational Goals

### Primary Learning Objectives

2. **Cultural Awareness**: Understand diverse traditions, foods, festivals
3. **Historical Context**: Learn about significant events and figures

### Content Guidelines
- âœ… **Show**: Inspiring stories of successful migrants
- âœ… **Show**: Reasons people migrate (economy, education, family)
